How to make a pizza:

  1. Buy ingredients
  2. Weight powder and water
  3. Mix the powder with water
  4. Play with a dough
  5. Press it into a shape you like
  6. Put your fav toppings on it
  7. Bake it for 30 mins
  8. Enjoy the pizza
